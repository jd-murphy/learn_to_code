Sum up that list!

Okay coder, we've seen some lists, now let's do some work with them! What about writing an online shopping application. We need a shopping cart for our customers to fill up with their purchases. How can we calculate the total when they want to check out?

total = 0.0
prices = [26.99, 13.00, 9.99, 9.99, 5.00, 27.50]
for price in prices:
  total += price
print(f"Your total is ${total}")
