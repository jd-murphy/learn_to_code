Multiply the fun!

Okay coder, we know Python can do addition and subtraction, but what if we need to multiply some numbers together? Well, with Python, it's easy peasy. Use the asterisk * to tell Python to multiply numbers. In your print() statement, let's multiply 10 and 32

print(10*32)

Run your code!

Look in the console and you should see

320

Check out some other multiplication problems like 6 multiplied by 14 or 32 multiplied by -1!
