Loop your name!

Okay coder, we can use for loops to iterate through other things as well. Let's loop through your name!

name = input("Enter your name, please: ")
for letter in name:
  print(letter)

We can manipulate the letters even! Try this

name = input("Enter your name, please: ")
for letter in name:
  print(letter.upper())

Loops are great for performing repetative tasks, and we will be using them all the time in our coding.
