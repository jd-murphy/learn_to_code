Hard working functions

Okay coder, we've seen how functions can work together. Let's take more of a look at the logic we can put inside functions. Let's write a function to find all factors of a number. Factors are numbers that multiply together to equal a number. For example, 2 and 5 are factors of 10. 1 and 10 are also factors of 10. There are no other integer factors that go into 10. So a list of factors of ten would be 1, 2, 5, and 10.

def find_factors(num):
  factors = []
  for i in range(1,num + 1):
     if num % i == 0:
      factors.append(i)
  return factors

print(find_factors(10))
print(find_factors(20))
print(find_factors(127))
print(find_factors(128))
